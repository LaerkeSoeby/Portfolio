Here's some of my favorite projects that I've created or co-created

I care about making great design combined with great usability.

Roll-Up for Papp Denmark

I designed a roll-up for print, in Adobe Photoshop for Papp Denmark. The most important factor for the roll-up was to be eye-catching, and getting people interested so they would come talk to Papp Denmark at the workshop. I chose to center the phone in the middle, as a large element, for it to be eye-catching and inform people that it's an app. Capital letters for "stop", to make people stop and think "why".

Website for It's Jive Time

I designed a website-page for web only, with HTML and CSS, for It's Jive Time. The most important factor for this website page was to be colorful and with a modern retro vibe. This page should show other peoples experience at the dance studio. I tried to balance the webpage with text at each side of the untraditional image, that breaks the navigation-bar in two. I those the four colored circles as an eye-catching element, because it's where the quotes from other dancers are written - therefor it should be easy to find.

Logo for BoligBoss

I designed a logo, in Adobe Illustrator for BoligBoss. The most important factor for the logo was the colors had to be the same as BoligBoss used in their app and we wanted the two B's to be the main focus. I wanted the logo to clearly represent that the content of the app dealt with managing a house yourself. Therefor I those the two B's to be connected by "pillars", in an almost three dimensional way. And added a roof, for people to associate the logo with a house.

Website for BoligBoss

I designed this part og the website for BoligBoss, in HTML and CSS, for mobile and web use. The most important factor for this side was that the functions of the app had to be displayed and with a short explanation. I tried giving it a professional look, but still playful, as the target group is the youth. With the four buttons at the start of the page, it's easy to click on the subject you would like to read more about, and the page scrolls down to the specific section for you.

Flyer for Papp Denmark

I designed a flyer for print, in Adobe Photoshop for Papp Denmark. The most important factor for this flyer was to be clear and easy to manage, they wanted it small, short and easy. I had to briefly inform the reader about what this app will be able to do, when it releases. For it to match the brand, I chose the pink background with a gradient. I wrote 3 short sentences about the app, and created a heading that should allure the reader to read more. The phone is photoshopped with a screenprint from the app "Papp", to be an eye-catching object.

Who am I, besides being Laerke Soeby Fenger?

Currently I'm studying multimediadesign and work with digital design, SoMe and communication on the side.

I am a creative and curious personality. And deeply fascinated by human psychology. Combined with my analyzing nature and natrual talent for fine motor skills, I'll consider myself a designer by heart.


Therefor my goal in design is creating design that not only looks good, but is easy to use and has a lot of thought behind it. The greatest thing about design is seeing your ideas becoming reality.

I tend to approach life with deep thoughtfulness & imagination.

That's probably why I'm the INFJ-A.

Schedule

It's important to me to have a detailed plan, and always be strategic when working on a project.

Everything has a meaning

I like being analytic and I always want to know why and how. I think it comes in handy when i comes to understanding the different target groups.

Being objective

Seeing multiple sides of the same case is natural to me. And i think it's important to see things from different perspectives to create balance.

Strive to be perfect

Being a perfectionist comes with challenges, but it also keeps me passionate.
